FIZZ BUZZ EXERCISE

Please enter two integers that we will refer to as FizzNum and BuzzNum. The FizzBuzz algo loops from 1 to 100 and every time through determines if FizzNum mod loop is 0 or BuzNum mod loop is 0. If either of those cases occur then we have a Fizz or a Buzz case. If both occur then we have a FizzBuzz case.
